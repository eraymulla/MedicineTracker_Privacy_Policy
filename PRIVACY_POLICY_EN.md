# Privacy Policy (Gizlilik Politikası)

**Last Updated:** January 27, 2025  
**App Name:** MedsMate / MedicineTracker (Medicine Tracker)  
**Version:** 1.0.1

---

## 1. Introduction

MedsMate / MedicineTracker ("We", "Us", "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes the information collected, used, and protected when you use our app.

This app stores your personal health information (medicine details, reminders) locally on your device and does not send it to any server.

---

## 2. Information Collected

### 2.1. Information Stored Locally

The app stores the following information **only on your device**:

- **Medicine information:** medicine name, dosage, frequency, start/end dates, notes
- **Reminders:** medicine times, taken/missed status
- **User settings:** notification preferences, theme, language
- **Onboarding status:** first-use information

### 2.2. Information Not Collected

The app **does not collect** any of the following:

- Personal identification (name, email, phone)
- Location data
- Device or advertising ID
- Data requiring internet connection
- Data shared with third-party services

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1. Encryption

All sensitive data (medicine and reminders) is stored using **hardware-backed encryption**:

- **iOS:** Keychain Services (Apple's secure storage system)
- **Android:** EncryptedSharedPreferences (Android's encrypted storage system)

### 3.2. Data Access

- Your data is stored **only on your device**
- Data is **not sent to any server**
- Data is **not shared with third parties**
- Data is **not used for advertising or analytics**

### 3.3. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time using the "Clear All Data" option in the app. Data is permanently removed from your device.

---

## 4. Notifications

The app uses **local notifications** for medicine reminders:

- Notifications are created **only on your device**
- Notification data is **not sent to any server**
- Notification permissions are **optional** and can be disabled at any time

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The app currently **does not use** the following third-party services:

- ❌ Analytics services (Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics, etc.)
- ❌ Crash reporting services (Sentry, Crashlytics, etc.)
- ❌ Advertising networks
- ❌ Cloud backup services
- ❌ Social media integrations

**Note:** If any of these services are added in the future, this policy will be updated and users will be informed.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

---

## 7. GDPR and Privacy Compliance

### 7.1. KVKK (Turkey)

This app is developed in compliance with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK):

- Your data is **not processed** without consent
- Your data is **not used** for purposes other than intended
- Your data is stored **securely**
- You have the **right to access** your data
- You have the **right to delete** your data

### 7.2. GDPR (European Union)

This app is developed in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Data minimisation:** Only necessary data is collected
- **Purpose limitation:** Data is used only for stated purposes
- **Storage limitation:** Data is kept only as long as necessary
- **Integrity and confidentiality:** Data is kept secure
- **Transparency:** Users know how their data is used

---

## 8. Your Rights

Under KVKK and GDPR you have the following rights:

1. **Right to be informed:** You can learn what data is collected
2. **Right of access:** You can access your data (view it in the app)
3. **Right to rectification:** You can correct incorrect data
4. **Right to erasure:** You can delete your data ("Clear All Data" in the app)
5. **Right to object:** You can object to data processing (not applicable as the app does not collect data for such purposes)
6. **Data portability:** You can export your data ("Export Data" in the app)

---

## 9. Data Export and Import

The app allows you to **export** and **import** your data in **JSON format**:

- **Export:** To back up your data or move it to another device
- **Import:** To restore a backup

**Important:** Exported data is in unencrypted JSON format. Store these files securely and do not share them with anyone.
